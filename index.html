about

I have been creating designs professionally for over 6 years. During that time, I have also worked as a web developer, product designer, strategist and manager on projects of different mediums and scope.

I am skilled at running workshops, gathering requirements, conducting user research, creating digital strategies, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, writing functional specifications, and web development. I also offer strong leadership skills, a working knowledge of Agile and Scrum frameworks (including running Sprint ceremonies), and experience in Google Analytics and SEO. 

While my skill set describes me as a generalist, I am a specialist in ensuring we are designing the right thing and then that we are designing that thing right.

I believe that strategy, UX and content go hand-in-hand when it comes to creating digital experiences that people actually want to use.
